Pricing FAQ

Clear boundaries for every plan

Does the AI publish my store automatically?

No. It creates drafts and guides you. You approve actions.

Can it connect to Shopify and Canva?

Yes. Where supported, it can help create safe draft assets after approval.

Can it help me choose pricing and payments?

Yes. It can generate pricing advice and payment setup guidance without collecting sensitive banking details.

Can it connect to marketing apps?

It can recommend apps and create drafts. Real connections and actions require your permission.

Can it buy domains or spend ad money?

No, not automatically. It gives guidance and requires approval for supported external actions.
