Does the AI publish my store automatically?

No. It creates drafts and guides you. You review and approve actions before supported safe changes are applied.

Can it connect to Shopify?

Yes, where supported, it can create safe draft assets after approval. Publishing remains your decision.

Can it help with Canva?

Yes. It creates branding and marketing briefs, plus Canva drafts where supported by the integration.

Can it buy domains or set up banking?

No. It guides you, but does not charge external services or handle sensitive banking details automatically.
