Business Setup Workflow

Follow this guided process to build a complete business foundation.

1. Business Idea

Define what your business does, who it helps, and what problem it solves.

2. Brand Name

Create a memorable business name, slogan, and brand identity direction.

3. Target Market

Identify your ideal customers, their needs, and where to reach them.

4. Products or Services

Plan your offers, pricing, packages, and product descriptions.

5. Marketing Plan

Create a social media, content, and launch strategy for your business.

6. Shopify Setup Plan

Plan your online store pages, products, collections, and launch checklist.

7. Canva Branding Plan

Plan your logo, colors, social media templates, and visual identity.

8. Launch Checklist

Generate a final checklist before launching your business publicly.
