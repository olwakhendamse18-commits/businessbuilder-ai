Beginner Guide

Connect Canva step by step

  1. Click Connect Canva.
  2. You will be redirected to Canva.
  3. Log in to your Canva account. Enter your login only on Canva's official authorization page.
  4. Review the permissions requested by BusinessBuilder AI.
  5. Click Allow or Authorize.
  6. Canva will redirect you back to BusinessBuilder AI.
  7. After a successful connection, generate Canva branding briefs and design drafts where supported. You review and approve drafts.

What the connection does: BusinessBuilder AI does not replace Canva. It helps you prepare brand direction, design briefs, banners, content ideas, and marketing assets that can be used inside Canva.

Use official authorization only. Only connect your own Canva account. Do not share your Canva login password with anyone. BusinessBuilder AI uses the official Canva connection flow and never asks for your Canva password directly.
How does Canva connection work?

Connect Canva sends you to Canva's official authorization page. After you review and approve access, Canva sends you back to BusinessBuilder AI. Your Canva password is not entered into BusinessBuilder AI.

Troubleshooting a failed connection
  • Check that your redirect URL is correct.
  • Make sure you are using the correct BusinessBuilder AI domain.
  • Try logging out of Canva and connecting again.
  • Make sure the Canva integration is enabled in your Canva developer settings.
  • If you see redirect_uri mismatch, add https://www.businessbuilder.site/canva_callback in Canva.
