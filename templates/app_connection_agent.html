Safety Rules

You control every connection and final action

  • No platform is connected without your permission.
  • BusinessBuilder AI never asks for platform passwords or displays stored keys.
  • Official OAuth or encrypted API-key flows are used where implemented.
  • No ad spend, ad publishing, mass email sending, or major website/store publishing happens automatically.
  • The AI creates drafts first. You review, edit, approve, and separately confirm supported actions.
