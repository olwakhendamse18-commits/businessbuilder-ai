FAQ

Common questions before you start.

Does the AI publish my Shopify store?

No. It can generate store packages and create safe draft assets where supported, but publishing stays inside your control.

Does Product Finder scrape Amazon?

No. It generates product research ideas, search suggestions, and research links only. You must verify prices, suppliers, stock, and policies.

Can it buy domains or set up banking?

No. It gives guidance and checklists, but domain purchases, billing, payment setup, and banking details must be handled directly by you.
